About

Sourced at origin,
shipped with proof.

We connect Tanzania's farms and aggregators to buyers around the world — with the grading discipline and paperwork that international trade demands.

Golden Seed Export is a Tanzanian agricultural commodities exporter built around a simple idea: buyers should know exactly what they're getting, and where it came from, before the cargo leaves port.

Tanzania's soils and climate produce world-class sunflower, cashew, sesame and garlic, alongside refined sugar moving through the region. We aggregate from trusted growers and cooperatives, grade against international standards, and handle the logistics through the Port of Dar es Salaam.

We work with importers, processors, distributors and wholesalers — from single-container trial orders to recurring multi-container programmes. Whatever the size, the process is the same: clear specs, available samples, and inspection-ready documentation at loading.

How we work

Tell us the commodity, grade, quantity and destination. We confirm availability and lead time, courier a sample if you want one, and issue a contract with agreed Incoterms. At loading we arrange phytosanitary certificates, a certificate of origin, and independent pre-shipment inspection so what you ordered is what you receive.
